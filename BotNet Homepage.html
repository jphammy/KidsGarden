Welcome to BotNet's interactive plant lab!

This website is broken into three sections: one for input, another for viewing the graphs, and the third one for
reading the user's and programmer's manual. These sections can be reached by the left hand navigation bar.

The input page is obviously allows the user to provide input from the data that they measured from the
expirement. The page will ask the user to give data for the following sections: height, light level, soil
moisture level, temperature, and humidity level.

The graphs section will send them to thingspeak.com where the student can login with their account,
locate their project, and view their graphs.

The last page includes links to the user's manual and the programmer's manual. The user's manual is a guide
used by the students to set up their devices for their lab. The programmer's manual is a guide used
by the teachers to understand how the device works and how to fix problems when they arise.

We thank you for choosing BotNet for your plant science experiment, and I hope you will find our services
useful and learn greatly from your experiments!
